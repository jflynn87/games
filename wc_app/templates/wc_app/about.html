World Cup Soccer/Football Game

Format

$25 entry fee per player, winner takes all (split in the case of a tie)

2 rounds of picks, Group Stage and Elimination Rounds

Points accumulate across both rounds, the high total score across both rounds wins

Pick Deadline will be the day of the start of each round (specific time to be determined by John's bedtime).

Failure to submit picks for the Elimination round will count as a DQ, any player failing to submit still owes the entry fee

Group Stage

Pick the finishing position of every team in their group

Points awarded for picking teams in the right finishing position. The finishing position is determined by records and several tie breakers. ESPN.com will be the source of all finishing positions

Pick qualifying team (#1 or #2 in the group): 3 points

Pick the group winner correctly: 2 bonus points

Pick 1-4 correctly for a group: 5 bonus points

The total points for a perfect group is 13 + any additional bonus points for an upset

Underdog bonus - if you pick the 3rd or 4th ranked team (based on the FIFA ranking) to finish in the top 2 and they do, the bonus is: (their world rank - #2 team world rank) *.3 - rounded to 2 decimal places

For example, if you pick Qatar to qualify there will be a 9.6 point bonus (50 - 18)*.3. Qatar is the 50th ranked team, Senegal is ranked 18 and is the second strongest in group A.

Knockout Stage

Bracket format, pick all winners before the stage begins.

Round 1: 5 points for correct picks

Round 2: 10 points for correct picks

Round 3: 15 points for correct picks

Consolation Round: 20 points for correct pick

Champion: 30 points for correct pick

No bonus points in the Knockout Stage
